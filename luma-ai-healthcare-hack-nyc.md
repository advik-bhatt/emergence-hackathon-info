## About

Come build the Conversation AI that healthcare actually needs. Healthcare Hack NYC is a
one-day, in-person sprint hosted with Arya Health — a Series A startup reimagining the
economics of healthcare services — at their NYC office. Main sponsor is Twilio's AI Startup
Searchlight, celebrating startups building the future of voice, conversational AI, and
LLM-powered agents. Bring a laptop, form a team, ship a real AI agent in a day.

## The Challenge

Build production-ready voice/text AI agents that can carry out a full conversation
end-to-end, grounded in domain knowledge and personalized to each caller. Reliability,
guardrails, security, and scalability are the prerequisite, not nice-to-haves.

**Must use Twilio to qualify for sponsor prizes.**

Suggested features:
- Telephony — must use Twilio to qualify for prizes
- Agent development framework — orchestrate/modularize conversation logic
- Model (TTS/STT) — best voice AI models
- Knowledge base — connect external sources/data for grounded context
- Caller info — recognize and personalize to the individual caller
